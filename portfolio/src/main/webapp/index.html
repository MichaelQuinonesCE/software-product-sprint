Hello, my name is Michael Quinones!

I am currently finishing my sophomore year under the science in computer engineering major. There's nothing more gratifying than going through the thorough process of working in groups in order to accomplish complex tasks. Stated this, I believe that the relationships and experience, a lot of hard work can be done and others can be benefited.
